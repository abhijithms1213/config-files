## Question :
- ######  *How to use pactl for unmute or mute sound !*  
## Explanatio**n**
---
- *pactl set-sink-mute @DEFAULT_SINK@ false*
	- will unmute it 
	- if need to be **muted** then,
		- use replace false with *true* at the end
