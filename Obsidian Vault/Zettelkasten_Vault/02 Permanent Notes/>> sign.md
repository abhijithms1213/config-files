- ######  *how to append a file's content without lossing original contect , using >> symbol*  
## Explanatio**n**
---
- *ls >> logTime.txt*
- *echo "next lines" >> logTime.txt*
- *whoami >> logTime.txt*
	- these all are append the content without using their original file content
