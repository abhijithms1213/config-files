- **UUID**
	- **UUID** stands for **Universally Unique Identifier**.  
	- It’s a 128-bit number used to uniquely identify something — like a disk partition, filesystem, or network interface — **across all systems**, not just one machine.	
	- if we unplug or plug the *ssd*  then may change `/dev/sda0` or *sda1* but *uuid* is unique
	- it also generated in file formates while we writing **mkfs.fat32** like
