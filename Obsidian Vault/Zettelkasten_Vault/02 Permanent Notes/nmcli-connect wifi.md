## Question :
- ######  *how to connect wifi with base nmcli tool?*  
## Explanatio**n**
---
- *for list wifi devices use below command*
```shell
nmcli device wifi list 
```
- it will list all wifi devices 
```zsh
nmcli device wifi connect name_01 --ask
```
- it will ask for password and enter it 
---
```ad-warning
if u not included --ask it may leads to error
```
